Why DocuGallery was built:
DocuGallery is built on June 15, 2026
DocuGallery was created to make it easy to upload documents such as PDFs, Word files, and text files, and then ask questions about their content using AI. It combines document retrieval with an AI language model so users can get accurate answers grounded in their own uploaded files, along with a general chat mode for everyday questions when no documents are uploaded.
